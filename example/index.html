ABOUT
Blending psychedelic rock, trip hop and desert blues, Atomic Fruit is a band that defies the divide between rock and electronic music.

With effects-laden guitars, dreamy vocals and mesmerizing grooves, the Berlin based four piece ensemble takes the listener on a journey through different soundscapes as their songs develop and evolve.
